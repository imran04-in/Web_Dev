Egg Bhurji (Indian-style scrambled eggs)

Egg Bhurji is a quick and flavorful Indian-style scrambled egg dish made with onions, tomatoes, and spices. It’s simple to cook, filling, and pairs perfectly with bread or roti.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 onion(chopped)
  • 1 tomato(chopped)
  • 2 green chilies(optional,chopped)
  • 2 tbsp oil or butter
  • Salt & pepper (to taste)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil/butter in a pan, add onions and green chilies, sauté until onions turn soft.
  2. Add tomatoes and cook until they become mushy.
  3. Break eggs directly into the pan, add salt and pepper
  4. Stir continuously until eggs are cooked and fluffy.
  5. Serve hot with roti, paratha, or bread.
